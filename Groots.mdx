## Groot offers three distinct bots

1. **Groot** – The classic music bot with all the essential features you need to keep the tunes flowing. Enjoy high-quality playback, easy queue management, and compatibility with your favorite platforms.

2. **Groot Flaky** – For those who want a lighter, no-fuss experience. Groot Flaky offers a streamlined music experience while maintaining the same reliability and fun. Perfect for smaller servers or minimalistic setups!

3. **Groot Premium** – Groot Premium is reserved for Premium Guilds, offering unparalleled features like 24/7 playback, custom audio filters, priority queueing, and exclusive dedicated support. Elevate your server’s music experience and enjoy the ultimate in quality and convenience!
